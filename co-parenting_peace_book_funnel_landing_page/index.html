My Journey

I’m Emma Hartley, and I know how heavy co-parenting can feel.

I remember the tension that came with every message, the emotional weight of handovers, and the quiet stress of always wondering how the next interaction would go. I tried explaining myself, staying calm, and doing everything “right,” hoping it would make things easier.

What I eventually learned was this: peace doesn’t come from changing the other parent. It comes from changing how much power conflict has over you.

This book wasn’t written to teach you how to win arguments or force cooperation. It was written to help you step out of emotional cycles that drain you — and to create a calmer, more stable environment for your child, regardless of how the other parent behaves.

I wrote the guide I wish I’d had — practical, steady, and focused on protecting peace rather than fueling conflict.

Emma Hartley

Author of The Co-Parenting Peace Manual
